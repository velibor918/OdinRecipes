Why Serbian Cuisine?

If there's one thing that holds true for all Serbs, from our worst to our best, it's that we're incredibly passionate. Every single aspect of our culture, from our laughs, to our fights, is fully founded in the inherent belief of absolute dedication. This is reflected in our cuisine: Unapologetically sensual, bountiful to the point of absurdity, it lets an individual reconnect with that which can't be said.
(Note:While some of these recipes may not have originated from Serbia, they have become such an integral part of our everyday life, that we wholeheartedly believe that they reflect the values of our culture.)
